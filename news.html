Hello Professor:

This page is to explain why I chose these colors and this design. I am a very unconventional person, from the movies I watch, the way I dress, and the music I listen to. Like I said in the about me page, I always wanted to live somewhere else, in a different culture, I don't know if you follow, but for me to be different from the rest is important. That is why I have been all these weeks working in making this page unique. I chose a different design, breaking color and other. Technology changes quickly, and I want to be part of that, even though I am just starting.

This past week as I was looking at the power point “Design for Conversion”, I realize I did not have any white space. I went to lynda.com and try some Edge animate for my home page, that way I give this page a bit of a white space, since my design did not had any. It is a table full of things I like; a map to remind me of places I have been, and places I want to go; coffee to keep me going and a reminder of home; and iPhone with the travel app; a passport, and reading glasses it reminds me of the ones I use when I study in the computer. Although my Edge Animate is so amateur, it was a quick and last minute impulse. I hope you like it, I know it has many defects and it could be better.
